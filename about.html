Hey! My name is Brian. I might not be a professional photographer, but I sure as heck like to shoot film. With no formal training, I rely mostly on intuition and drawing influence from friends that are serious photographers. One of my greatest influences happens to be a friend of mine by the name of Noorann. I met Nooran through some other friends, and was fortunate enough to meet her while visiting in Baltimore. If you are interested in seeing Noorann's works, which have been recognized in university classrooms and large publications, follow the link below!
